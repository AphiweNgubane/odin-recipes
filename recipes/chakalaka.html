Chakalaka

Description

Chakalaka is a traditional South African relish that mainly contains tomatoes, beans and onions. You can add your choice of veggies like carrots and peppers to make the perfect 7 colours dish. Pair it with ujeqe, rice, or just as a salad side at a braai.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 2 green bird's eye chillies (optional)
  • 2 clove garlic, crushed
  • 50g ginger, finely grated
  • 2 tbsp mild curry powder
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 5 large grated carrots
  • 400 grams chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 400 grams canned baked beans
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • salt and freshly ground black paper

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pan, then fry the onions until soft and translucent.
  2. Add the chiles, garlic and half of the ginger.
  3. Add the curry powder and stir to combine.
  4. Mix in the bell peppers and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in the carrots and make sure everything is well combined.
  6. Add in the tomatoes and tomato paste then stir.
  7. Cook until the mixture is well combined and slightly thickened.
  8. Remove from the heat and add the baked beans, thyme and remaining ginger.
  9. Stir to combine, season with salt and pepper, then enjoy hot or cold!
